Chicken Mayo Sandwich

This is a recipe on making a chicken mayo sandwich. You will need two slices of bread and some chicken slices. Put the chicken slices on one slice of the bread.

Once that is done, put the other slice of bred on top of the chicken slices to sandwich them

Thats it. You are done!

Ingredients

  • Chicken slices 500g
  • Two slices of Bread
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  1. Place one slices of bread on chopping board
  2. Place the chicken slices on the slice
  3. Place the second slice on top of the chicken
