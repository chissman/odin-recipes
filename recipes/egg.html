Egg Recipe

This is how to prepare Eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Break the egg in a bowl and add one or two pinches of salt
  2. Place pan on stove and put 20mls of cooking oil
  3. When oil is mildly heated pour the egg onto the pan
  4. When egg starts turning slightly brown, turn it to the other side until moderate brown
